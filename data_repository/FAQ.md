# FAQ

## Plans and Pricing

Business plan includes unlimited videos with 1080p exports and SSO.

Pro plan supports up to 100 videos per month with 720p exports.

Free plan allows 10 videos with watermarked 480p exports.

## Upload Limits

Standard upload limit is 2GB per file via web interface.

For files larger than 2GB, use the Desktop app or split the file.

Business customers can request higher limits by contacting support.

## Account Management

SSO is available for Business plans only.

You can change your plan anytime from the Settings page.

Cancellation is immediate, no refunds for remaining billing period.
