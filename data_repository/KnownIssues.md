# Known Issues

## Video Processing

Occasional delays in processing videos over 1 hour in length.

Rare audio sync issues with certain video formats - workaround: re-encode source.

Export failures may occur with very high resolution source material (4K+).

## Browser Compatibility

Safari has limited support for web-based uploads over 500MB.

Chrome recommended for best performance on all features.

Firefox users may experience slower export generation.

## Mobile Apps

iOS app requires iOS 14 or later.

Android app may have playback issues on devices with less than 2GB RAM.

Mobile uploads limited to 500MB without WiFi connection.
